Aroha Hauora is a gamified self-care and planner app designed to help you optimize efficient time management with visual planners, task categorization, timers, and working spaces – while taking care of your mental health with meditative and therapeutic features inspired by Pacific, Māori, and Asian cultures.

✨ Why Aroha Hauora?

Everything You Need, Beautifully Integrated

Here are some key features we are currently developing!

Dashboard

Your Daily Command Center

See your productivity streak, active quests, AI chat, mini-games, and available rewards – all in one beautiful, customizable dashboard. Earn "Mana" points as you complete tasks and unlock real-life rewards like cafe vouchers.

Productivity

AI-Powered Quest Queue

Our AI Command Center helps you summarize tasks and break down quests. Watch your progress in real-time with the Quest Queue, track completion percentages, and stay motivated with time estimates.

Mindfulness

Cultural Discovery Hub

Start your daily Waka journey to center your morning intentions. Take mood checks, set preferences, and explore brain games like "Swipe Your Vibe", "Pack Your Kete", "The Waka Journey", and "Mauri Balance" – all rooted in indigenous Polynesian cultures and Buddhism.

Companion

Meet Kori the Kiwi

Chat with Kori, your interactive AI companion, to schedule quests, discover what motivates you, and get personalized recommendations. Kori grows with you as you accumulate points and achievements through your journey.

Wellness Game

Pack Your Kete

Identify the top things slowing you down and drag them into your basket. Let our AI analyze your hurdles and provide personalized strategies to overcome barriers like fatigue, fear of failing, or phone distractions.

❤️ About the Team

AROHA HAUORA

Spreading love and wellbeing to everyone

We are a YES company from Manurewa High School, consisting of 4 students from Polynesian and Asian backgrounds (Cook Island, Vietnamese, Chinese, Nepali).

We aim to spread Aroha (Love) to the communities Hauora (Wellbeing) with our services and products. Our mission is to create technology that is culturally meaningful and genuinely helpful.
